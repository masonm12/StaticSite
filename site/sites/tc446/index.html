Mathew Mason - Bio

I'm currently a double major student at Michigan State University (see left), working towards a career in software engineering, and video game development. I'm a native of Michigan, and it's my hope (though not a realistic one) to find a job here. When that fails, I plan to desperately seek out any job I can making games, where I will probably work hundreds of hours per week.

In my spare time I enjoy playing games with friends, both locally and over the internet, though more often than not I can't find enough time to keep my head above the sea of twelve year old, master-chief demi-gods flooding the net. I make games at an amateur level, and hope to soon make some programs of a quality to enter into some festivals around the country. I'm involved in Spartasoft, MSU's official game design club, and outside of that I work 2 jobs. I can't say that design is my favorite aspect of development, as I tend to think more programmatically, but I understand that it's an important aspect, and hope to improve my aesthetic eye in the future.
