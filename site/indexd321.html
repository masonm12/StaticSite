First!!1!1

Posted by Mathew on August 23rd, 2008


Well, this is my first "real" post, and it's fairly exciting stuff. At the time of writing the site is still in beta (well, alpha really) and doesn't include much content or art, but is still probably the most advanced web project I've ever worked up.

I know the odds of more than, say, a dozen people reading this are slim, yet I still feel the experience of making my own blog styled site is valuable, and I hope that in the future it will encourage me to not only work harder on my various projects in order to get them posted here, but will also facilitate some environment for me to lay out my thoughts in front of no particular audience.

Here's to hoping for the best.
